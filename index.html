About Us:

Shastri Associates dates back to 1971 when Dr. Ganesh Ganapati Shastri started a small Advocate chamber at Vyalikaval, Bangalore, with a few colleagues. Now Sri. Sadanand Ganesh Shastri, B.A.(L), LL.M, PGDIPRL has taken over the reins of Shastri Associates since his completion of law degree and enrolled in the Karnataka State Bar Council from the year 2009.
