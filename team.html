Our Team

Mr. Sadanand Shastri is a Second-Generation Advocate. Since last 14 years he is practicing in the Hon'ble Supreme Court, The Hon'ble High Court of Karnataka and Trial Courts of Bangalore. He heads Shastri Associates and has handled several Civil, Criminal, Labour, Family and Service matters.

Mr. Vinay Hosmath is a Second-Generation Advocate and has put in 15 years of practice under the able tutelage of Sri. D.S. Hosmath, Senior Advocate. Though Hosmath’s are managing their own Law Offices, Vinay being a classmate and good friend of Mr.Sadanand Shastri has collaborated with Shastri Associates. He brings in tremendous experience and ease of business of High Court Appeals and Medical Council matters. His forte is overwhelming in Appeal Matters and has been a rightful addition to Shastri Associates.

Mr. Chetan is an asset to Shastri Associates and is the go-to person for all Real Estate matters. He brings in 10 plus years of experience in revenue documents and registrations and is been with Shastri Associates for last 4 years.

Mrs. Mane is from Bagalkot Karnataka, and is a first generation Advocate. She handles all litigation matters in criminal and civil field. She can easily touch base with all rural litigants and has been a valuable addition to Shastri Associates.
